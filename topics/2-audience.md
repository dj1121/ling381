# Knowing Your Audience
This week we will focus on getting to know the language of your field. The writers in each field
all have their own ways of being persuasive and conveying meaning. In other words, they have their own ways of saying,
"This is important." In order to become a successful academic writer in your field, it will pay off to know these
and incorporate them into your own voice.

## Main Ideas
- Describing your work
- Knowing your field’s lingo
- What is persuasive to your audience?
