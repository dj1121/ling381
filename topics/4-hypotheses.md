# Hypotheses
Research is all about proposing and testing new ideas. Fields differ on what sorts of hypotheses
they can present and if/how they are testable. However, what I recommend always is to state your hypothesis as soon as possible
and let the rest of the paper flow around it. This week, we will practice stating hypotheses in our writing.

## Main Ideas
- Hypothesis generation and testing
- Deductive, inductive, and abductive methods
- Wording your hypothesis
- Remembering to explore alternatives
