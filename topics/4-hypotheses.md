# Hypotheses

## Main Ideas
Research is all about proposing and testing new ideas (i.e., hypotheses). Fields differ on what sorts of hypotheses
they can present and if/how they are testable. However, what is common to good academic writing is to make your hypothesis
(or hypotheses) clear from the start and let the rest of the paper flow around it. This week we will practice explaining our
hypotheses in our writing.
