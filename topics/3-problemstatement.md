# Problem Statement
Now that we know your audience, it's time to get them hooked. Readers appreciate when you get to the point clearly and quickly. 
Have you ever heard the phrase, "Less is more"?
When it comes to writing, especially with highly technical papers, this idea can really help make a paper easier to read.
This week, we will learn about using less words to convey more meaning and quickly establishing the problem you will address.
This will make your introductions far more enticing to the reader. 

## Main Ideas
- Grabbing the reader
- Getting the essence/gist
- Concise meaning making
- Cleaning things up
