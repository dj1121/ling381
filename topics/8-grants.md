# Grant Proposals
All researchers eventually need to write grant proposals in order to get funding. This week, we will 
build on the abstract writing from last week and turn it into a grant proposal. We don't have time
to write a full research paper in this class, but a grant proposal will serve as very good practice for
all of the things we've learned. Our goal will be to practice writing a proposal for the [TGS GRG](https://www.tgs.northwestern.edu/funding/fellowships-and-grants/internal-fellowships-grants/graduate-research-grant.html).

## Main Ideas
- Planning the proposal
- Level of technicality
- Purpose/Feasability
