# Less Is More
Readers appreciate when you get to the point clearly and quickly. Have you ever heard the phrase, "Less is more"?
When it comes to writing, especially with highly technical papers, this idea can really help make a paper easier to read.
This week, we will learn about using less words to convey more meaning.

## Main Ideas
- Summarization
- Concise meaning making
- Cleaning things up
