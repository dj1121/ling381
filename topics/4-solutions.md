# Present Solutions
Research is all about proposing and testing new ideas (hypotheses), but fields differ in this process.
I recommend you be aware of what hypotheses/solutions you are entertaining, as this will promote clarity in your writing.

## Main Ideas
- Research processes
- Wording your hypothesis/solutions
- Remembering to explore alternatives
